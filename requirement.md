Create a modern, AI-powered Life Planning & Execution Platform — a premium productivity system that helps users turn life vision into daily action.

The product should combine long-term planning, short-term execution, task management, habit tracking, journaling, analytics, and AI coaching into one seamless system.

Think of it as:
Notion + TickTick + Motion + Obsidian + Apple Journal + Sunsama
but redesigned from first principles for deep life planning, execution clarity, and personal growth.

Core philosophy:
“Turn life goals into executable systems.”

The app should help users:
- define their life vision
- set yearly goals
- break yearly goals into quarterly plans
- convert quarterly plans into monthly outcomes
- convert monthly outcomes into weekly execution systems
- convert weekly systems into daily actionable tasks
- track progress automatically
- reflect and improve consistently
- receive AI guidance, coaching, and planning support

The product should feel premium, minimal, calm, visual, and deeply focused.
It should not feel like a crowded task app. It should feel like a personal operating system for life.
